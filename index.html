FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a serviced apartment?

All Amanthos Living apartments include a fully equipped kitchen with appliances and cookware, free high-speed Wi-Fi, Smart TV, regular professional cleaning with linen changes, and digital check-in with keyless entry. Depending on the location, you may also have access to parking, air conditioning, and stunning views.

How much cheaper are you vs hotels?

Our serviced apartments typically cost 50% less than equivalent hotel rooms in the same area, while offering 2-3x more space with a full kitchen, laundry, and all amenities included. Use our booking search to see real-time rates for your dates.

How does check-in work?

We offer fully digital, self-service check-in. Before your arrival, you'll receive detailed access instructions via email, including your digital key code. There's no need to coordinate with anyone or wait at a reception. Simply arrive and let yourself in.

What is the cancellation policy?

We offer two rate types: Refundable rates allow free cancellation up to the specified deadline (usually 24-48 hours before check-in). Non-Refundable rates offer the best price but cannot be cancelled or modified. You can choose your preferred rate type during booking.

Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available at all three locations. At Zurich Airport, private parking is available for CHF 10/day. At Solothurn and Nyon, on-site parking options are available. Please check the specific location page for details.

Can I book for business travel?

Absolutely! Our apartments are ideal for business travelers. Every unit includes a dedicated workspace, high-speed Wi-Fi suitable for video conferencing, and a fully equipped kitchen. We can provide invoices for company expense reporting.
