2. Overview of Data Processing

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP), applicable cantonal data protection regulations, and this privacy policy.

When you use this website, various personal data may be collected. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

3. Data Collection on This Website

3.1 Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files, which your browser transmits to us. These include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL
  • Hostname of the accessing device
  • Time of the server request
  • IP address (anonymised)

This data is not merged with other data sources. The basis for data processing is our legitimate interest in ensuring the secure and efficient operation of our website.

3.2 Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses essential cookies for functionality. We also use localStorage to save your language preference. We do not use tracking cookies. When you first visit our site, a cookie consent banner will inform you about the use of cookies.

3.3 Contact and Booking Forms

When you contact us via email or use our booking form, the data you provide (name, email address, phone number, booking details) will be stored and processed for the purpose of handling your inquiry or booking. We will not share this data with third parties without your consent, unless required for the fulfilment of your booking (e.g. with our property management system).

4. Analytics

This website uses Google Tag Manager to manage website tags. Google Tag Manager itself does not collect any personal data. The tool triggers other tags that may collect data. However, Google Tag Manager does not access this data. If deactivation has been made at the domain or cookie level, it will remain in effect for all tracking tags implemented with Google Tag Manager.

5. Booking and Payment

When you make a booking through our website, we collect the information necessary to process your reservation, including:

  • Name and contact details
  • Check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests
  • Payment information (processed securely via our payment provider)

Payment transactions are handled by third-party payment processors. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers.

6. Your Rights

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request information about your stored personal data at any time.
  • Right to rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, unless we are legally obligated to retain it.
  • Right to data portability: You can request that we transfer your data to you or another controller in a commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your personal data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@amanthosliving.com.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmissions.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For displaying web fonts. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Tag Manager: For managing website tags. See section 4 above.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this privacy policy, or as required by law (e.g. tax and accounting obligations). Booking data is retained for the duration required by Swiss law.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version is always available on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
